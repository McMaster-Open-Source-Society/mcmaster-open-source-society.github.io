About Us

The McMaster Open Source Society (MOSS) is a student-run club dedicated to fostering collaborative software development and practical coding experience through real-world open source projects.


Whether you're a beginner looking to get started with GitHub or an experienced developer eager to build something impactful, MOSS provides a casual and inclusive space to learn, contribute, and grow.


We believe in open collaboration, flexible schedules, and meaningful code, all without the pressure of constant meetings or in-person requirements.


Join us to level up your skills, boost your GitHub profile, and work on projects that actually matter.
